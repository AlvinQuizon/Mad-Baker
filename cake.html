Cakes

A cake is a delightful dessert that is typically made from a mixture of flour, sugar, eggs, butter or oil, and other flavorings. It is baked to perfection and often layered or frosted to create a visually appealing and scrumptious treat. Cakes come in various shapes, sizes, and flavors, catering to different tastes and occasions. Whether it's a classic chocolate cake, a luscious strawberry shortcake, or an intricately designed wedding cake, these delectable creations bring joy and celebration to birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, and many other special events. With their enticing aroma, moist texture, and irresistible taste, cakes are a centerpiece of dessert tables, leaving lasting memories and satisfying the sweet tooth of cake enthusiasts worldwide.

Chocolate Celebration Cake

About the bake

If you are looking to elevate your everyday chocolate cake recipe this indulgent chocolate celebration cake is the recipe for you. With the inclusion of Billington's Unrefined Light Muscovado Sugar in the chocolate sponge your cake will have a unique richness of flavour that will really give it the wow factor. Soft moist sponge, creamy buttercream, topped with a silky smooth ganache this cake is ideal for a big celebration.

Click Here: Method & Ingredients

Ingredients
For the Cake

175g Dark chocolate

200g Butter (unsalted)

200g Self-raising white flour

300g White Sugar

30g Cocoa powder

1tsp Baking powder

4pcs Eggs


For the Buttercream

250g Icing Sugar

30g Cocoa powder

80g Butter (unsalted, softened)

25ml Milk (whole)


For the Chocolate Ganache

200g Dark chocolate

200g Double cream

200g Malteasers

Utensils

Measuring cups and spoons.

Stand mixer.

Large mixing bowl.

Doughnut cutter

Oven

Wire rack

Tongs, wooden chopsticks, or spider.

Method

Step 1:
Preheat the oven to 180°C (170°C fan, gas mark 4). Put some water in a cake tin or roasting tin and put in the base of the oven. Line 2 x 20cm sandwich tins with baking parchment.

Step 2:
In a small pan set over a very low heat melt together the chocolate and butter with 100ml water. The mixture will be smooth and glossy.

Step 3:
Sift together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder and baking powder. Mix in the eggs until well blended. Stir in the chocolate and butter. Spoon into the prepared tins and smooth the top.

Step 4:
Bake for 30 minutes, until a skewer comes out clean when inserted. Remove from the tin and set aside on a wire rack to cool.

Step 5:
Meanwhile, make the buttercream by beating together the icing sugar, cocoa powder, butter and milk. Fill the two sponges, sandwiching them together and use the remaining buttercream to cover the sides and top of the cake.

Step 6:
To make the ganache, heat the dark chocolate and cream together over a low heat until it becomes smooth and glossy. Carefully pour on to the top of the cake and with a pallet knife smooth to cover the top and coax some drips down the side of the cake.

Step 7:
Finish by decorating with Malteasers and a cake topper.

Total time: 35mins
Prep Time: 20mins
Bake time: 15mins
Serving: 12

Chocolate Cupcakes

About the bake

This foolproof Mary Berry chocolate cupcake recipe is pure chocolate indulgence. Children will love to decorate with chocolate strands and chocolate decorations.

In just 35 minutes you'll have made a batch of 12 delicious chocolate cupcakes.

Click Here: Method & Ingredients

Ingredients

250g    Self-raising white flou

0.5tsp; Baking powder

40g     Billington's Unrefined Golden Caster Sugar

2tbsp  Milk

1pc   Medium Eggs

2tbsp  Milk

150ml  Vegetable oil


For the Dip

150g Dark chocolate chips

150ml Double cream

Utensils

Measuring cups and spoons.

Stand mixer.

Large mixing bowl.

Doughnut cutter

Oven

Wire rack

Deep fryer

Tongs, wooden chopsticks, or spider.

Method

Step 1:
Sift together the flour and baking powder into a bowl and stir in the sugar. Beat together the eggs, oil and milk then add to the dry ingredients. Mix together until a smooth dough forms.

Step 2:
Beat one tablespoon of oil and egg into into the dry ingredients, adding the milk slowly until the mixture forms a soft dough.

Step 3:
Heat the remaining oil in a deep-fat fryer to 170°C (335°F) or until a little of the mixture sizzles as soon as it is dropped in.

Step 4:
Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured work surface and pat out to about 1 cm thick. Cut into thin strips and place carefully into the hot oil. Fry for 3-4 mins on each side, until golden and crisp.

Step 5:
Sprinkle the doughnuts with the sugar and cinnamon while hot and serve as soon as possible.

Step 6:
To make the chocolate dip, gently warm the chocolate and cream together until melted. Pour into a serving dish and serve

Serving: 1

Chocolate Mug Cake

About the bake

Good things don't always come to those who wait...

Have you only got 5 minutes to spare? Need to satisfy your sugar cravings but your cupboards are bare? Never fear, our Chocolate Mug Cake has come to the rescue. This quick mug cake recipe can be filled and topped with your favourite chocolate and is sure to be gooey, rich and ready to eat in less than a couple of minutes.

Click Here: Method & Ingredients

Ingredients

500g    Self-raising white flour

1tsp    Baking powder

90g     Billington's Unrefined Golden Caster Sugar

2tbsp  Milk

2pcs   Medium Eggs

2tbsp  Sunflower oil

Utensils

Measuring cups and spoons.

Stand mixer.

Large mixing bowl.

Doughnut cutter

Oven

Wire rack

Deep fryer

Tongs, wooden chopsticks, or spider.

Method

Step 1:
Place the flour, sugar and cocoa powder in a microwaveable mug and stir together until well mixed.

Step 2:
Stir in the oil and boiling water and stir together until the mixture is smooth.

Step 3:
For the Nutella mug cake: Spoon the Nutella into the middle of the mixture. For the Biscoff mug cake: Spoon the Biscoff Spread into the middle of the mixture. For the Lindt mug cake: Push the Lindt Lindor Truffles into the middle of the mixture.

Step 4:
Spoon the cake mixture over the filling so it’s concealed.

Step 5:
Microwave for 1 minute* until the cake is risen and has started to pull away from the edge of the mug.

Step 6:
Top the mug cake with delicious decorations, grab a spoon and dig in!
